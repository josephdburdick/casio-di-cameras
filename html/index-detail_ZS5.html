Optical Zoom

Image Precision Beyond The Optical Zoom

Only Casio could find a way to pack a 12.5X optical zoom lens, a large 3.0-inch monitor and a CMOS shift stabilization mechanism into a slim, compact camera body. Equipped with a ultra-wide angle starting at 24mm (35mm film camera equivalent) all the way up to a 12.5X telephoto zoom (300mm).
