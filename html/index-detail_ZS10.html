ZS-10

The new ZS10 with slim, elegantly design fits into your life with ease: Thanks to the 26 mm wide-angle lens with 5x optical zoom, the camera captures sweeping landscapes as well as group photos from a short distance and details in close-up shots. The short shutter delay allows you to take spontaneous snapshots - the camera is quickly ready to take the next photo. Equipped with 14.1 megapixels, Easy Mode and HD video recording for high-resolution and detailed videos, the compact lifestyle camera makes for easy-to-shoot, breathtaking pictures every time - and a great deal of fun.

Optical Zoom

Image Precision Beyond the Optical Zoom

Only Casio could find a way to pack a 12.5X optical zoom lens, a large 3.0-inch monitor and a CMOS shift stabilization mechanism into a slim, compact camera body. Equipped with a ultra-wide angle starting at 24mm (35mm film camera equivalent) all the way up to a 12.5X telephoto zoom (300mm). With Casio's High-Speed SR Zoom technology the EX-ZR100 extends the range to an amazing 25X (600mm) allowing the photographer to be ready for any scene!
