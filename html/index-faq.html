Why won't my camera turn on?

Please check all of these items which you may not have included:

  1. Is battery present?
  2. Is battery fully charged?
  3. Is battery inserted correctly? (Are the arrows aligned?)
  4. Is battery correct type or manufacturer?
  5. Are cradle and AC adapter or charger unit working correctly?
    (If you are using a model with a cradle)
  6. Is USB port on computer working correctly? Try another USB port or another computer.
  7. Did you insert your memory card?

Please remove the card and test camera again. If this step restores operation of your camera, you probably have a defective memory card. Please test or replace card per the card manufacturer's recommendation.

My camera works okay in play mode, but in record mode the screen goes black after a few seconds, what's wrong?

This symptom may occur in cameras with an optical viewfinder. These cameras have a Power Save feature that turns OFF the display in RECORD mode after about 2 seconds. Simply press the DISP button to cycle this mode OFF or ON.

When I put my camera on the cradle, why does
the charge light:

  1. Not come on? Answer: Battery missing or AC adapter not plugged in.
  2. Blink red? Answer: Battery defective.
  3. Never turn green? Answer: Battery defective.
